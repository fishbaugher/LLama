Seasons

The autumns come and change the trees,
Set fire to the horizon.
The winters come with freezing breeze,
And snow the kids will slide on.
The springs come blooming drenched in rain,
And each day grows new hope.
Then summers come and we dwindle
Into dehydrated sweat bags of nope
